About Me

My journey into AI started with a fascination for what's possible when technology and creativity intersect. This same drive that leads me to build complex technical systems is inspired by my interests in music and art. It's about finding harmony in the data and a clear vision in a sea of complexity. Professionally, I'm a hands-on AI/ML Engineer specializing in the design and development of multi-agent AI systems. My experience ranges from crafting a finance and reporting AI agent to building document-parsing systems with memory and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). My focus is on creating practical, production-ready AI tools that directly solve business problems for startups. I believe in modular, scalable architectures and the power of intelligent automation. If you're a founder or investor and want to talk about how AI agents can transform operations, drive innovation, or simply save you from 'spreadsheet hell,' I'd love to connect.
